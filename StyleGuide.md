###Abbreviations

When in doubt, don't.

1) Do not abbreviate streets, boulevard, avenue, drive, and road.

2) Use Mr., Mrs., Ms., Dr.

3) Do not abbreviate position titles (e.g. Governor, Mayor, Senator, Representative), except in headlines.
